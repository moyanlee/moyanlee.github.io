Who created it?

Nasty Nets was a by-proudct of the website Del.icio.us, a social link-sharing platform

Founded in 2006, Nasty nets followed the tragetory of Del.icio.us developing into a collobartive blogging website that focused on collecting and archiving decontexualized online media.

What is it?

Nasty Nets was a collaborative blogging site where users would post content from all corners of the web. The platform serves as a precursor to some of the more centralized platforms we see today (Twitter, Facebook, Tumblr). Similar to these platforms, users could interact through commenting and making follow-up posts. Posts varied from web-screenshots and advertisements to alternations and collages of found-media. The lo-fi aesthetic coupled with material created by amature users of the site made Nasty Nets a site for artistic expression thorough digital means.
